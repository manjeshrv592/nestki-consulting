Services for Environmental Management

Our top priority at Nestki is to meticulously plan the environmental impact of every one of our initiatives. When we consider initiatives, sustainability comes to mind. In order to achieve the twin objectives of project excellence and sustainability, we collaborate closely with our clients.

We have been managing almost all facets of environmental planning and sustainability, from regulatory compliance to green buildings, by drawing on our knowledge spanning more than ten years. Our primary areas of focus are environmental compliance management, solid and hazardous waste management, environmental impact assessments with management plans, environmental site assessments (ESA) phases I and II, environment, water, and energy auditing, environmental and social due diligence studies, ISO & OHSAS advisory services, environmental monitoring, social impact assessments, including R&R and RAP studies, source water venerability and source water protection plans, water optimization, and environmental compliance management. We have completed more than 450 projects, all of which have passed the strict environmental requirements.
