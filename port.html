Port & Shipping

The development of a nation's economy as a whole and the maintenance of trade and commerce growth depend heavily on the ports and shipping sector. The exchange of products between nations has greatly increased as a result of the globalization of the international economy. As a result of the advent of economies of scale and the advancement of shipping technology, global trade has also increased. Ports in every nation will undoubtedly continue to play a crucial and significant role in offering the most affordable form of transportation to handle the ever-expanding global trade.

The Sagarmala strategic effort by the Indian government will undoubtedly increase trade on a national and international level and serve as a key driver for the country's expanding economy. Numerous projects in the areas of port modernization and new port development, port connectivity improvement, port-linked industry, and coastal community development have been identified under the Sagarmala Programme for phase-wise implementation between 2015 and 2035.

In order to provide excellence on all fronts of port development projects, from site selection and pre-feasibility studies through engineering, procurement, and project management assistance, Nestki has assembled a strong team of professionals with the necessary skills. Our team has the distinct benefit of having a wealth of knowledge in the development of greenfield port projects from idea to commissioning, as well as in advising and planning services. Nestki has the special benefit of housing all the industries and subject matter experts needed for the Sagarmala initiative under one roof. Nestki will be able to offer value-added services to its clients as a result.
