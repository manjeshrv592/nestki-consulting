Transportation, Roads & Bridges

A nation's infrastructure cannot be separated from its transportation system, which supports social and economic advancement as well as the movement of an increasingly mobile population and goods. In terms of project management, engineering, planning, and design for road infrastructure, Nestki is in the forefront. We work together with clients to establish effective, cost-effective, and safe transportation systems that support the growth and development of the country.

Our transportation specialists offer concept to commissioning services to Concessionaires, EPC clients, and Governments (GoI and State Governments) across the globe. We firmly think that we are in a unique position to conduct various investigations, planning, design, and project management of linear transportation projects fulfilling timetables, targeted prices, and quality requirements because we have successfully completed difficult projects. We assist with the restoration and reorganisation of outdated systems in addition to creating new ones.
