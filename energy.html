Energy

Nestki possesses skills in pre-dispatch material inspection on a national scale, strategic planning, power system analysis, power quality, project feasibility development, pre-bid engineering, DPR preparation, detail design, and construction supervision.

We contributed to the construction of SEZs in numerous sectors with areas ranging from 25 acres to 3000 acres dispersed throughout all regions of India with our expertise in core design and engineering. Prior to putting the design up for tendering, our Teams closely collaborated with the majority of the states' power utilities to examine and approve the design, including the detail execution drawings. In order to determine the T&D losses in the existing distribution system and to prepare DPRs for 164 towns in order to modernise and upgrade the system with new technologies in order to reduce both technical and commercial losses, Nestki has extensive experience in these areas.

In order to design EHV lines and Substations up to 220 kV, we have a dedicated staff for extensive profile surveys. Our expertise in transmission and distribution includes designing new infrastructure and altering existing infrastructure, as well as making substation enhancements and designing significant electrical transmission infrastructure. Solutions for energy transmission and distribution are offered by our engineers to customers throughout Africa, the GCC, and India. Reduced transmission and distribution losses, cost-effective and cutting-edge IT building designs, and the planning and construction of electrical networks and sub-stations in Africa have all been made possible thanks to our expertise.

We assist our clients in developing complete efficiency programmes to reduce energy consumption, develop renewable resources, including grid resilience, and reduce loss through the use of the most recent technologies.
