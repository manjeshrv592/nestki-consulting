Financial, legal, and contract services

A decision to engage in an activity is always supported by the potential results. With a beneficial outcome anticipated, activity is carried out but otherwise avoided. So, before starting a project, it is determined whether it will be viable and compliant with contractual and regulatory requirements. In terms of viability, we evaluate the proposal using financial and/or economic criteria. We support the Client throughout the entire project, including pre-bid services, agreement, execution, and commissioning.

Nestki has extensive internal domain knowledge of infrastructure projects at all stages because it offers concept to commissioning services throughout the project life cycle. As a result, we provide advice services such as market analysis, financial and economic viability checks, project structuring, RFQ/RFP preparation, bid process management & partner selection, concession agreement preparation, and other legal papers, among others. This sets us apart from other providers of financial advising, contractual, and legal services.

Our skilled team of professionals has the necessary training and expertise to support our clients in making wise decisions. We bring all pertinent factors together so the client can weigh the benefits and drawbacks of the project. For prospective, current, and under pressure infrastructure projects, we offer consultancy services.
