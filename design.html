Design and Project Management

The foundation of tomorrow's constructed environments is architecture. Excellent design influences how people live their lives, enhances the built environment, and contributes to resource conservation. Nestki's mission is to simplify this complexity, create outstanding functional spaces, and offer building complexes that are affordable, well-built, and inspirational. These improvements will make living easier and of higher quality. For the past 20 years, Nestki has been organising and creating complicated projects.

Our architects strive to offer cutting-edge design options while strictly adhering to budgets, timelines, and legal requirements. Green and sustainable architecture themes are fully integrated into our building and interior designs. Residential areas, commercial complexes, multiplexes, office buildings, institutions, universities, and industrial spatial forms are all included in our in-house design capabilities. Our design philosophy allows us to provide unique and useful local solutions. The Design Team takes great delight in producing iconic designs that give the Project its personality. The designs are produced taking into account all aspects of sustainability, green building characteristics, GRIHA rating, IGBC rating, and Vaastu rules as necessary.

Nestki takes pleasure in offering our clients value through expert project management services and overseeing the timely and cost-effective completion of their projects. For our foreign clients, including those in Malawi, Rwanda, Cape Verde, Georgia, Bangladesh, Kenya, Gabon, Burkina Faso, etc., our value engineering initiatives have reduced time and costs.
