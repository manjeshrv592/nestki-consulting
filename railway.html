Railway Infrastructure

For rail projects, Nestki offers a wide choice of environmentally friendly options, including project management and construction oversight, rail electrification, rail structures, rail infrastructure, and more.

Senior experts who have worked for the Indian Railways and have relevant experience in the entire system of design, construction, and project management of formation, bridge structures, permanent way, electrification, signalling, and telecommunication are among the wide range of professionals at Nestki.

For railway projects like surveys of new lines, doublings, and gauge conversion, geometric design and checking of route alignment and yards, and integrated planning, design, and construction supervision of civil, electrical, signalling, and telecommunication networks, Nestki has the most up-to-date design, drafting, and construction supervision capabilities.
