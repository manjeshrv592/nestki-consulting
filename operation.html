Services for Operation and Maintenance

In order to guarantee a smooth working environment and extend the life cycle of machinery and infrastructural facilities, operation and maintenance (O&M) services use a systematic approach to safety, health, the environment, dependability, and asset efficiency

Nestki has extensive internal domain knowledge of infrastructure projects at all stages because it offers concept to commissioning services throughout the project life cycle. As stated in the SOW or any authorised O&M Plan, we offer the customised solutions needed to run, maintain, and track the effectiveness of the asset. From immediate supervision to continuing, hands-on administration of your complete utility infrastructure, our specialists are qualified to handle it all.

For the operation and upkeep of your infrastructure and utility systems, Nestki may offer any number of qualified, experienced on-site staff members. We can offer anything from a single operator technician to a full operations and maintenance team with knowledge of each segment.
